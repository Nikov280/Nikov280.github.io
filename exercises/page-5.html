Welcome to Hook Head Lighthouse

Explore Ireland's Oldest Operational Lighthouse

Guided Lighthouse Tours

Step inside the iconic Hook Head Lighthouse and journey through centuries of maritime history with our expert guides.

Learn about the lives of lighthouse keepers, the evolution of navigation, and the architectural marvel of this medieval tower.

Panoramic Coastal Views

Climb to the top for breathtaking views of the rugged coastline, crashing waves, and the vast expanse of the Celtic Sea.

Perfect for photographers, nature lovers, and anyone seeking a moment of awe and tranquility.

Marine Wildlife Watching

Spot seals, dolphins, and seabirds from the lighthouse grounds, especially during the warmer months when marine life is most active.

Interpretive signs and binoculars are available to enhance your wildlife experience along the scenic walking trails.
