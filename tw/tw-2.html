Lionel Messi

Considered one of the greatest players of all time, Lionel Messi has won a record seven Ballon d'Or awards and led Argentina to World Cup victory in 2022.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Portuguese superstar known for his incredible athleticism, goal-scoring prowess, and five Ballon d'Or awards with multiple top European clubs.

Kylian Mbappé

French forward known for his explosive speed and clinical finishing, winning the World Cup with France in 2018 at just 19 years old.

Kevin De Bruyne

Belgian midfielder renowned for his exceptional passing range, vision, and technical ability, instrumental in Manchester City's success.

Erling Haaland

Norwegian striker known for his incredible goal-scoring record, physical presence, and clinical finishing with Manchester City.

Virgil van Dijk

Dutch defender considered one of the best center-backs in the world, known for his leadership, aerial ability, and defensive prowess.

Karim Benzema

French striker known for his technical ability, link-up play, and clinical finishing, winning the Ballon d'Or in 2022 with Real Madrid.

Robert Lewandowski

Polish striker renowned for his goal-scoring consistency, positioning, and technical skills with Bayern Munich and Barcelona.
